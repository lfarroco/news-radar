OpenAI expands with new office in London

OpenAI, the leading artificial intelligence research organization, is excited to announce the opening of its first international office in London, United Kingdom. This expansion signifies a significant milestone in OpenAI's growth and commitment to broadening the scope of its operations.

With a focus on bringing in diverse perspectives and accelerating the mission of ensuring that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity, OpenAI aims to build dynamic teams in research, engineering, and go-to-market functions in London.

"We are thrilled to extend our research and development footprint into London, a city globally renowned for its rich culture and exceptional talent pool," says Diane Yoon, OpenAI's VP of People. "We are eager to collaborate with the local community and reinforce our efforts in creating and promoting safe AGI."

London's vibrant technology ecosystem and exceptional talent make it the ideal location for OpenAI's first international office. The teams in London will focus on advancing OpenAI's leading-edge research and engineering capabilities, contributing to the organization's mission.

This expansion presents exciting opportunities for developers in the UK and beyond to engage with OpenAI's work and contribute to the development of AGI. Stay tuned for more updates from OpenAI London as they continue to make strides in the field of artificial intelligence.

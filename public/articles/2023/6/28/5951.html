Fly.io, a startup focused on optimizing app performance, has recently announced that they raised a total of $95MM in funding. The funding round was led by A16Z and EQT Ventures, with participation from existing investors such as Intel Capital and Dell. This significant investment will allow Fly.io to further develop their platform and expand their services.

The company's mission is to bring apps closer to their users, improving performance and user experience. They believe that certain types of apps, such as video streaming and real-time presence applications, require physical locality for optimal performance. Their platform utilizes technologies like WebRTC, edge caching, and game servers to achieve this goal.

Fly.io's success in securing substantial funding demonstrates the growing demand for solutions that enhance app performance. Developers who are interested in optimizing their applications and staying ahead of the competition should keep an eye on Fly.io and their innovative approach to app delivery.

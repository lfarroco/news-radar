Flask, a popular Python web framework, has been used to build a free AI chatbot that allows users to interact with PDF documents. This innovative chatbot aims to improve efficiency by providing quick access to information within documents. With support for CSV, PDF, and TXT files, developers can easily upload and analyze various types of documents.

The chatbot offers several useful features. Users can ask the bot to identify any fishy or harmful content within a document, making it ideal for reviewing terms and conditions pages. Additionally, the bot can summarize entire documents, saving users time and effort. For developers, the chatbot can even provide specific guidelines by uploading tutorial documents and requesting code snippets.
